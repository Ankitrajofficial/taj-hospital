About us

Healthcare and education under one roof

Located on the Bettiah–Areraj highway in Jagdishpur, Taj Hospital & Paramedical Science is dedicated to providing accessible, affordable, and quality healthcare to the people of Bettiah and surrounding areas.

Alongside patient care, our institute imparts quality education to give students a competitive advantage — with a higher rate of success and ample opportunities in both private and government sectors.

  • 24×7 emergency & ambulance service
  • Experienced doctors and qualified staff
  • On-site diagnostics, lab & pharmacy
  • Accredited paramedical training with placements

Good to know

Frequently asked questions

Quick answers to what patients and students ask us most.

Are you really open 24 hours?

Yes. Our emergency and casualty services run 24×7, every day of the week. For non-emergency OPD timings, please call 099314 52682.

Do I need an appointment to visit?

Emergencies are attended immediately, no appointment needed. For OPD consultations and health packages, booking ahead helps reduce your waiting time — use the form below or call us.

What documents should I bring?

Please carry a valid ID, any previous prescriptions or reports, and your insurance/scheme card if applicable.

How do I apply for a paramedical course?

Admissions are open for the new session. Send an enquiry through the form below or call us, and our admissions team will guide you on eligibility, fees and documents.

Do you provide an ambulance?

Yes, a 24×7 ambulance service is available for safe and quick patient transport. Call 099314 52682 for assistance.

Medical emergency?

Our team is ready 24×7. Don't wait — call us right now.
